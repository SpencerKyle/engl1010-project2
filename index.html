Welcome to a Safe Space <3

This site acts as a service for all things pride! We welcome anyone who wishes to learn more about what 'pride' means and hope to help serve as a community hub for you. To be 'pride' is to understand inclusion and see everyone on this earth as a loving individual. Whether you self-identify as lesbian, gay, bisexual, queer, transgender, questioning (or even just an ally!) then this is the perfect place for you. We hope to provide you with a wide enough range of information and resources that you will be able to go on and enrich the lives of others! You are welcome to invite any of your friends and family to check out this site!

Our Mission

Empower and enrich the lives of students who identify as a part of the LGBTQIA+ community.

Terminology

LGBTQIA+

an acronym representing the diverse spectrum of sexual orientations and gender identities, including lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, queer/questioning, intersex, and asexual, with the "+" indicating that it's an inclusive term for other identities not specifically listed.

Coming out

Coming out refers to the process of accepting your sexual orientation or gender identity and share that identity openly with other people.

Ally

Someone who actively supports and advocates for the rights and well-being of LGBTQ+ individuals and communities, often those who are not part of the LGBTQ+ community themselves.

Gender Expression

how a person outwardly presents their gender, encompassing behaviors, clothing, hairstyles, mannerisms, and other characteristics that convey gender.
